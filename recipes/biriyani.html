Biriyani

Biriyani, a spicy chicken and rice dish, is one of the most well-known Indian-Pakistani dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Â½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 small green chile peppers

Directions

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in yogurt and vinegar.
  2. Add onion; cook and stir until golden and tender. Add tomatoes, chile peppers, garlic, and ginger paste.
  3. Cook and stir until tomatoes are tender. Season with garam masala, mint, salt, and pepper. Stir in cilantro.
